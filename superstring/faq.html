I want to change the audio track on the lyrics video.

You can Replace your audio track. If you want to keep the old one, please Duplicate first and Replace it.

I can't type in my native language.

Some fonts may not support your language. If the text does not appear correctly, the font doesn't support that language. You can find supported languages below.

  • Arabic: El Messiri, Sukar, Thabit
  • Chinese(Simplified): Noto Sans, Noto Serif
  • Chinese(Traditional): Noto Sans, Noto Serif
  • Devanagari: Teko
  • Greek: Alegreya, Alegreya Sans, GFS Neohellenic, Vollkorn
  • Gujarati: Rasa
  • Japanese: Godo, Nanum Barun Pen, Noto Sans, Noto Serif
  • Korean: Dohyeon, Godo, Gmarket Sans, Hanna, Jalnan, Nanum Barun Pen, Nanum Brush Script, Nanum Square Round, Noto Sans
  • Latin: Alegreya, Alegreya Sans, Alfa Slab One, Amatic SC, Arvo, Bangers, Bebas Neue, Bitter, Cinzel, Courgette, Dohyeon, Damion, El Messiri, Fondamento, GFS Neohellenic, Godo, Graduate, Gmarket Sans, Hanna, Jalnan, Josefin Sans, Josefin Slab, Kanit, Lobster Two, Megrim, Metal Mania, Nanum Barun Pen, Nanum Brush Script, Nanum Square Round, New Rocker, Noticia Text, Noto Sans, Noto Serif, Pacifico, Peace Sans, Permanent Marker, Pridi, Racing Sans One, Rasa, Righteous, Rock Salt, Sukar, Teko, Thabit, Uncial Antiqua, Underwood Champion, Vollkorn, Wellfleet
  • Sinhala: Abhaya Libre
  • Thai: Kanit, Pridi
  • Vietnamese: Kanit, Vollkorn

Can I use my own fonts?

Unfortunately, Superstring does not support to use custom fonts in your computer. However, we continue to add fonts to Superstring every update. We strive to diversify our selection to give our users as many choices as possible. If you have fonts you want us to add, simply contact us and we will do our best to add it to Superstring.

Error: "The publisher of Superstring couldn't be verified."

While downloading Superstring, if you are getting an error "The publisher of Superstring couldn't be verified." or "The publisher of this program couldn't be verified", please try to download it again using a different browser like Chrome or Firefox.

What happens if I don't renew my license?

If you don't want to renew your license after it expires, you can continue to use the version of Superstring that you have, for as long as you want, but you won't be able to receive any further updates. If you decide you want to renew at a later date, you're welcome back at any time.

When will I have to renew my license?

Your license is valid for 1 year after the date of purchase so if you bought your license today {{site.time | date: "%b %-d, %Y"}}, you'll get all the regular updates that are released up to {{site.time | date: "%b %-d"}}, {{site.time | date: "%Y" | plus:1}}. To receive updates after that date, you'll need to renew your license.

Can I install Superstring on multiple computers?

When you purchase Superstring, your license entitles you to use Superstring on one computer only.

Refunds for licenses

To get a refund for a license purchase, contact us with:

  • The email address you used for purchasing.
  • The license key you would like to have refunded.
  • The reason for refund.

Please note: Our refund policy is designed to help with incompatibility — not as a way to use our apps for free. We reserve the right to reject a refund if our policy is being abused.
